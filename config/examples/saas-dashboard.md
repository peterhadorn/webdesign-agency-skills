## Design Context

### Users
Internal team members and power users managing data daily. Used for hours at a time — efficiency and data density matter more than visual delight. The job: "Show me status, metrics, and actions at a glance."

### Brand Personality
Utilitarian, data-focused, professional. Internal tool — clarity and speed over aesthetics.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark theme for long sessions. High-contrast text on dark backgrounds. Color coding for status and categories (functional, not decorative). Compact layouts. System font stack. Minimal decoration. Data tables, badges, and compact cards.

### Design Principles
1. **Data density over whitespace** — Every pixel earns its place
2. **Color means something** — Status colors are functional, not decorative
3. **Fast interactions** — No animations that slow workflow
4. **Consistent patterns** — Same components everywhere
5. **Keyboard-friendly** — Power users don't reach for the mouse
