## Design Context

### Users
Potential customers evaluating your product or service. They visit on mobile, scan quickly, and decide based on trust signals and first impressions. The job: "Can I trust this business to solve my problem?"

### Brand Personality
Professional, trustworthy, confident. Each brand is unique — replace this default with your specific brand personality.

### Aesthetic Direction
Bold, distinctive, anti-generic. Full-viewport heroes with commanding typography. Distinctive fonts (never Inter/Roboto/system defaults). Generous section spacing. Dark sections where brand colors pop. Atmospheric backgrounds with depth.

### Design Principles
1. **Bold over safe** — Distinctive fonts, aggressive brand color application, atmospheric backgrounds
2. **Trust through specificity** — Real testimonials, real credentials, real contact info
3. **Mobile-first, content-always** — Never hide content on mobile, reorder instead
4. **One clear CTA per section** — Don't make visitors think about what to do next
5. **Show, don't tell** — Images, videos, and social proof over marketing copy
