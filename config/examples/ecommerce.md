## Design Context

### Users
Shoppers comparing products and making purchase decisions. Often on mobile, often distracted. The job: "Find the right product, trust the store, and check out without friction."

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, efficient, visually appealing. The store should feel curated and reliable — not overwhelming or cheap.

### Aesthetic Direction
Clean, product-focused. Large product images. White or light backgrounds that don't compete with products. Accent colors for CTAs and promotions only. Clear price display. Trust badges visible. Fast-loading, image-optimized.

### Design Principles
1. **Product is hero** — Photography quality and size matter more than layout tricks
2. **Trust at every step** — Reviews, ratings, shipping info, return policy visible throughout
3. **Frictionless checkout** — Every extra field or step loses customers
4. **Mobile-first always** — Most shopping happens on phones
5. **Speed is conversion** — Every 100ms of load time costs sales
